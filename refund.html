Refund Policy

CoopCash by PalconAI Technologies Private Limited

📅 Effective: March 2026 🇮🇳 India
01

Free Trial

CoopCash offers a 60-day free Pro trial to all new users. No payment is required during the trial period. You can explore all Pro features at no cost.

No credit card is required to start your free trial. You will never be charged automatically.
02

Subscription Payments

CoopCash Pro subscriptions are processed through Google Play Store. All payments are subject to Google Play's billing terms and conditions.

  • Monthly plan: billed every 30 days
  • Yearly plan: billed once every 12 months
  • Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled before the renewal date
03

Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds in the following cases:

  • You were charged after cancelling your subscription due to a technical error
  • You were charged twice for the same billing period
  • The app was completely non-functional for the majority of your billing period due to a bug on our side

Refund requests must be made within 7 days of the charge.

04

Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • You forgot to cancel before the renewal date
  • You no longer use the app but the subscription is still active
  • You did not use the Pro features during the billing period
  • Partial month or partial year usage
  • Change of mind after purchase
05

How to Cancel

To cancel your CoopCash Pro subscription:

  • Open Google Play Store on your Android device
  • Tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions
  • Find CoopCash and tap Cancel subscription

Your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period. You will not be charged again after cancellation.

We never delete your farm data when you cancel. All your records remain safe and accessible when you resubscribe.
