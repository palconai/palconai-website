Delete Your CoopCash Account

You can delete your CoopCash account and all associated data directly from within the app.

Steps to delete your account:

  1. Open the CoopCash app
  2. Go to Profile (bottom navigation)
  3. Tap Settings
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm deletion

⚠️ This will permanently delete your account and all farm data. This action cannot be undone.

If you need help, contact us at support@palconai.com
