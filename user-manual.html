Getting Started

CoopCash is a farm management app for Indian broiler farmers. It helps you track expenses, sales, mortality, and profits — all in one place.

Download & Install

Download CoopCash from the Google Play Store. Search for "CoopCash" or visit the link on our website.

Sign Up

  1. Open CoopCash and enter your mobile number.
  2. Enter the OTP sent to your number.
  3. Enter your name and select your district.
  4. Choose your preferred language (Telugu, Tamil, Malayalam, Hindi, Kannada, or English).
  5. You are now on the Standard plan. You can start a 60-day free Pro trial from the home screen.
Tip: You can change your language anytime from Settings → Language.
02

Managing Batches

A batch represents one cycle of broiler farming — from chick placement to final sale.

Starting a New Batch

  1. Tap + New Batch on the home screen.
  2. Select your location and shed.
  3. Enter the number of chicks, price per chick, and placement date.
  4. Optionally enter chick company and breed details.
  5. Tap Start Batch to begin.
Tip: The placement date can be set up to 7 days in the future if you are planning ahead.

Batch Status

  • Active — Batch is ongoing. You can add expenses, sales, and mortality.
  • Completed — All birds have been sold. Final P&L is calculated.

Standard Plan Limits

Standard plan allows 1 active batch at a time. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited active batches.

Archiving & Restoring

Completed batches can be archived to keep your list clean. You can restore them anytime from the archived batches view.

03

Recording Expenses

Track all costs for your batch — feed, medicine, vaccines, labour, and more.

Adding an Expense

  1. Open your batch and go to the Expenses tab.
  2. Tap a category card (Feed, Medicine, Vaccine, Labour, Other) or tap + on any card.
  3. Enter the expense details — amount, date, and notes.
  4. For feed expenses, enter number of bags, bag size, and price per bag.
  5. For medicine/vaccine, you can optionally enter medicine name and company.
  6. Tap Save.

Scan a Bill

Tap Scan Bill when adding an expense to automatically read the amount, date, and details from a photo of your bill. Works with printed and handwritten bills in Telugu and English.

Tip: The chick purchase expense is automatically created when you start a batch. Tap the chick card to edit it.

Bulk Delete

Check the Select All checkbox to select multiple expenses and delete them together. The chick expense cannot be deleted.

04

Recording Sales

Record each sale as birds leave your farm. Multiple traders can be recorded per batch.

Adding a Sale

  1. Open your batch and go to the Sales tab.
  2. Tap + Add Sale.
  3. Enter number of birds sold, average weight per bird, and rate per kg.
  4. Enter the trader/buyer name and sale date.
  5. Tap Save.

Multi-Trader Sales

You can record multiple sales to different traders. Each sale is numbered S#1, S#2, etc. The app calculates weighted average rate and total revenue automatically.

Scan Sales Bill

Tap Scan Bill to automatically fill in sale details from a trader's bill. You can scan multiple bills at once and review before saving.

Tip: When all birds are sold, the batch automatically completes and final P&L is calculated.
05

Recording Mortality

Track bird deaths daily to keep your mortality percentage accurate.

Adding a Mortality Entry

  1. Open your batch and go to the Mortality tab.
  2. Tap + Add Mortality.
  3. Enter the number of birds that died and the date.
  4. Optionally enter the reason.
  5. Tap Save.

The mortality percentage is automatically updated on the batch summary card.

06

Growth Tracking

Track bird weight over time to monitor growth against target.

Adding a Weight Record

  1. Open your batch and tap Add Weight on the batch card.
  2. Enter the average weight in grams or kg.
  3. Select the date of weighing.
  4. Tap Save.

The Growth tab shows a chart of actual weight vs target weight over the batch days.

Feed Phase Tracker

The feed phase card shows your current phase (PBS, BS, or Finisher), recommended feed bags, and how many bags you have used so far.

07

Market Prices

View live broiler market prices across all states and districts in India.

  • Tap Market Prices from the home screen.
  • States are grouped and collapsible. Andhra Pradesh is expanded by default.
  • Switch between Prices and Trend tabs to see price history charts.
  • A 🔼 or 🔽 indicator shows if tomorrow's price is expected to change.
08

Farm Insights

Farm Insights gives you a smart overview of your entire farm's performance. Available on Pro plan.

Overview Tab

Shows total birds, total revenue, total expenses, and net P&L across all batches.

Charts Tab

Donut charts showing expense breakdown per batch — Feed, Chicks, Medicine, Labour, and Other as percentages of total spend.

Health Tab

Flock Health Score per batch based on Mortality %, FCR, and weight vs target. Shows 🟢 Healthy, 🟡 Monitor, or 🔴 At Risk status.

09

PDF Reports

Generate and share professional PDF reports for your batches.

Batch Report

  1. Open a completed batch.
  2. Tap the download icon (⬇) in the top right.
  3. The PDF is generated with summary, sales details, and feed details.
  4. Share via WhatsApp, email, or save to your phone.
Note: Completed batch PDF is available on Standard plan. Ongoing batch PDF requires Pro.

Farm Statement

From Farm Insights, tap Statement to generate an all-time farm P&L statement across all batches.

Plans & Subscription

Standard Plan (Free)

  • 1 location, 1 shed
  • Unlimited batches (1 active at a time)
  • Completed batch PDF reports
  • Market prices & full history
  • Basic insights

CoopCash Pro

  • Unlimited locations & sheds
  • Multiple active batches
  • Farm Insights (Overview, Charts, Health)
  • Ongoing batch PDF reports
  • Scan Bill feature
  • Priority WhatsApp support

Free Trial

New users can start a 60-day free Pro trial. Tap Try Pro free for 60 days on the home screen banner, or tap any Pro feature to begin your trial.

🎉 Launch offer: ₹199/month or ₹1,999/year (regular ₹299/month or ₹2,990/year)

How to Upgrade

  1. Go to Settings → Standard Plan → tap Go Pro ✨
  2. Or tap any Pro-gated feature.
  3. Select Monthly or Yearly plan.
  4. Complete payment via Google Play.

How to Cancel

To cancel your subscription, go to Google Play Store → My Subscriptions → CoopCash → Cancel Subscription. Your Pro access continues until the end of the billing period.

Your Data After Cancellation

We never delete your farm data. If you cancel Pro, your data is locked — not deleted. You can resubscribe anytime and access all your records.

For refund queries, see our Refund Policy.

Account & Settings

Edit Profile

Go to Settings → Edit Profile to update your name or district.

Change Language

Go to Settings → Language to switch between Telugu, Tamil, Malayalam, Hindi, Kannada, and English.

Manage Locations & Sheds

Go to Settings → Manage Locations & Sheds to add, edit, or remove farm locations and sheds.

Delete Account

To permanently delete your account and all data, visit palconai.com/delete-account.html or go to Settings → Delete Account.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your farm data will be erased.
